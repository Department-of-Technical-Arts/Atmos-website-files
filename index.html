WHY ONE SHOULD BECOME A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR
  • 1.A chance to better one’s communication skills by interacting with a diverse set of people.
  • 2.An opportunity to learn more about tech-related events.
  • 3.An opportunity to boost your profile.

RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Publicity - You’ll be expected to publicize about the fest in your college and if your college receives posters, ensure that they are put up on your college notice board. Share ATMOS publicity material on social media groups (Eg. Facebook).
  • Co-ordination - Provision of contacts like that of HODs or college president/secretary.
  • Contingent - The campus ambassador is expected to get in a contingent of at least 10 people and register them as a single contingent only, when you come to the fest

WE HAVE PLENTY OF INCENTIVES IN STORE
  • 1. .A BITS Atmos T-shirt will be given along with a certificate of organization as Campus Ambassador.
  • 2. Registrations will be exempted for the Campus Ambassador for all the three fests if the contingent is above 30.
  • 3. More Atmos goodies at a discounted price.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS
  • 1. The Campus Ambassador has to send photos of posters put up on notice boards(if posters are sent) and screenshots of social media publicity to publicrelations.bphc@gmail.com.
  • 2.The Certification of Organization as Campus Ambassador will be given only if he fulfils the necessary conditions and is deemed fit by the organizers.
